HMZ BMI Calculator is a general tool for estimating body weight healthiness based on height and weight only and may not be accurate for athletes, children, the elderly, or pregnant women.

Consult a healthcare professional for a comprehensive health assessment and personalized medical advice. Use it as a guideline, not a definitive measure of health.

Primary Uses:

  1. Screening: BMI is often used as a screening tool to categorize individuals into different weight categories. This helps in identifying potential health risks associated with each category.
  2. Health Risks: High or low BMI values can indicate increased risks of various health conditions, eg:
    • Underweight: Can be associated with malnutrition, osteoporosis, and weakened immune function.
    • Overweight and Obesity: Can be linked to higher risks of heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, and certain cancers.
  3. Tracking Changes: BMI can be used to monitor changes in body weight over time.
  4. Guiding Interventions: Based on BMI results, healthcare providers can recommend appropriate lifestyle changes, dietary adjustments, and physical activity to help individuals achieve a healthier weight.
